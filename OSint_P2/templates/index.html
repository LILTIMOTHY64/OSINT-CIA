About This Tool

This OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) analyzer collects and analyzes publicly available information from multiple platforms to determine the overall sentiment around a specific keyword or topic.

  • Reddit: Analyzes posts and discussions
  • Google News: Scrapes recent news articles
  • RSS Feeds: Collects from major news sources

Uses VADER and TextBlob for sentiment analysis • Generates detailed visualizations and reports
